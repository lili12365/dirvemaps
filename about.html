About the site

About DriveMads

DriveMads is an independently maintained browser game site focused on quick play, source disclosure, and a smaller catalog that can be reviewed page by page. The site is operated through the DriveMads editorial desk, and the primary public contact address is hello@drivemads.online.

Who runs the site

DriveMads is run as an independent online publishing project. The same editorial desk that selects or removes game pages also handles content corrections, privacy questions, copyright requests, and day-to-day maintenance through the monitored site inbox.

What gets published

The site publishes browser game landing pages that combine a playable third-party embed with original copy about controls, gameplay flow, mobile limitations, source disclosure, and related internal links. DriveMads does not claim ownership of those third-party games unless a page explicitly says otherwise.

How pages are reviewed

Before a page stays in the main catalog, the review pass checks whether the game still loads, whether the source host is clearly identified, whether the surrounding text has real value without the iframe, and whether the internal links still point to live pages.

How maintenance works

The catalog is revisited when embedded sources change, when a game becomes unavailable, when privacy or ad behavior changes, or when a page is judged too thin compared with stronger alternatives. We would rather shrink the catalog than keep low-value pages indexed.
