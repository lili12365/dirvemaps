Privacy Policy

Last updated on Friday, July 17, 2026. This page explains what DriveMads collects directly, which third-party services are in use, how embedded games behave, and how visitors can allow, reject, or change optional cookies and ad settings.

Who this policy covers

This policy applies to pages published on drivemads.online. DriveMads is operated through the DriveMads editorial desk. Privacy questions can be sent to hello@drivemads.online.

Data the site can collect directly

Like most websites, DriveMads and its hosting or CDN layer can receive technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, requested URL, referrer, date and time, and response status. That information is used for delivery, security, abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and aggregate performance monitoring.

Cookies and local storage used by DriveMads

DriveMads stores your cookie choice in browser local storage so the site can remember whether you accepted or rejected optional analytics and advertising storage. If you reject optional storage, the site still works, but optional Google Analytics and Google AdSense loading stays off until you change that choice.

Google Analytics

DriveMads uses Google Analytics 4 with property ID G-ZME7DCP4MK to measure page views, engagement, catalog performance, and broad traffic patterns. Analytics is treated as optional storage on this site and is only enabled after consent through the site banner or privacy settings control.

Google AdSense

DriveMads uses Google AdSense with publisher ID ca-pub-3376278485410551 to deliver and measure ads. AdSense loading is also optional on this site and depends on your consent choice. If you allow advertising storage, Google and its partners may use cookies or similar identifiers for ad delivery, frequency control, measurement, and ad personalization where legally permitted.

Embedded game providers

Game pages on DriveMads use third-party embeds or source pages from providers that currently include drivinggames.io, azgames.io, game.azgame.io, and gamea.azgame.io. When you load a game iframe or open a source link, that provider can receive your IP address, browser information, and other technical identifiers in the same way it would if you visited the provider directly.

How to manage or block optional tracking

You can reject optional storage from the DriveMads consent banner, reopen those controls at any time through the button below, block third-party cookies in your browser, use private browsing modes, or use an ad blocker or tracking-protection tool. For Google ad personalization controls, you can also review the settings available through your Google account and browser privacy features.

What DriveMads does not currently offer

DriveMads does not provide user accounts, checkout flows, or a live public comment system on the current build. Visitors can still contact the editorial desk directly by email if a page needs to be corrected or removed.

Policy updates

This page is updated when the site adds or removes material services, changes the consent flow, changes advertising behavior, or materially changes how embedded content is handled. The current effective version is dated Friday, July 17, 2026.
