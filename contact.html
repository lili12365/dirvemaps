Contact DriveMads

DriveMads is operated through one monitored editorial inbox. Support questions, corrections, rights requests, and privacy issues can all be sent to hello@drivemads.online.

Support and content corrections

Use the site inbox to report broken embeds, wrong source links, control notes that no longer match the current version, or pages that should be removed from the main catalog. Include the exact URL and a short explanation of what needs attention.

Copyright and removal requests

If you are a rights holder or authorized representative, send the affected page URL, the material in question, your relationship to that material, and the action you want us to take. The editorial desk reviews removal requests case by case and can disable pages or source links when necessary.

Privacy and consent questions

Questions about cookies, analytics, ad behavior, or consent choices should also go to the same inbox. If your message is about a privacy setting, mention whether you were using the homepage, a specific game page, or the privacy policy page when the issue happened.

Business and partnership messages

Brand, sponsorship, or content-partnership proposals should include the company name, the relevant game or section, and a practical reason the request fits a curated browser game site instead of a bulk ad inventory buy.

What to include

  • Your name, studio, or company
  • The exact page URL
  • A concise description of the problem or request
  • A working reply email address
  • Any deadline or legal context if relevant

DriveMads is run by the DriveMads editorial desk. All public contact currently routes through the site inbox so requests are logged in one place and can be reviewed consistently.
